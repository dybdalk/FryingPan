Privacy Policy
As a supporter and consumer of Out of the FryingPan, your security and information privacy are vital aspects of our business. Please view our privacy policy below for more information.

We collect and store all information given to us by you. This includes all information requirred for transactional/shiping purposes, IP address, and all account information. Technical and statistical data pertaining to the function and business of the site are also collected using software scripts.
How we use your information
We use the collected information to provide our services, protect your information, and to improve our business. Statistical information such as time spent on pages, mouse clicks/hovers, location, etc. will be used to further marketing and business research of the company.

Information needed to perform your transactions, such as shipping address, can be accessed by our trusted third-party partners and contractors. Information may also be shared in the good belief that it will prevent possible security or safety issues. Information will also be provided when complying to the law. Information Security
Much of your personal information is encrypted using SSL and HTTPS. The employees and partners who work with or have access to your personal information arealso under contractual agreement pertaining to information privacy. Under no circumstances will your personal information be released for third-party commercial purposes.
